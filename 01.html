    Healthy Pumpkin Bread

    TIME

    16 Mins

    Servings

    Serves: 12

    Diet Restriction

    - - -

    Rating

    0 Votes

    Like this recipe & share with friends!

    Secret Ingredients

    • 1 1/4 cups canned pumpkin
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 1/2 cup brown sugar
    • 1/2 cup water
    • 1 cup applesauce
    • 1 egg
    • 2 egg whites
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 2/3 cup whole wheat flour
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

    Directions

    1. Heat oven to 350.
    2. In large bowl, combine pumpkin, sugars, water, applesauce, and eggs. Beat until well mixed.
    3. Combine flours, baking soda, baking powder, salt and spices in a separate bowl. Stir until combined.
    4. Slowly add dry ingredients to pumpkin mixture. Stir until well combined.
    5. Grease a 9x5 loaf pan and dust with flour. Pour batter into pan.
    6. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until cooked through and golden brown.
    7. Cool for 5 minutes and remove from pan. Cool on cooling rack until fully cooled.
